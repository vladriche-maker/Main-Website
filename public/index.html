The consultant behind the wardrobe.

I'm Vladimir Riché — a personal style consultant working exclusively with high-performing professionals in finance and law at a pivotal moment in their careers: the day they make partner.

That promotion changes everything. The meetings you're in, the clients you're facing, the authority you need to project. Most people dress for the job they had. I help them dress for the one they just earned.

My approach is precise, private, and built entirely around your life — your firm's culture, your client base, your schedule, your existing wardrobe. I don't do generic style guides. I do the work.

Why capsule wardrobes for partners.

A capsule wardrobe isn't minimalism for its own sake. It's a decision you make once — so you never have to think about it again. For partners billing at your level, mental bandwidth is the most valuable thing you have.

The right wardrobe becomes invisible in the best possible way. It just works. Every meeting. Every client. Every room.

The Partner-Ready Edit

A focused wardrobe engagement for the newly named partner in finance or law.

"You don't need more clothes. You need the right ones — chosen once, worn with confidence, for years."

Making partner is one of the most significant professional milestones of your career. But the wardrobe most partners arrive with was built for an earlier version of their role — and it shows.

The Partner-Ready Edit is my entry engagement: a high-precision capsule wardrobe built specifically around your new reality. Your firm. Your clients. Your life outside the office. Done.

What's Included

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

The edit covers every professional context you operate in, built to work together as a coherent system — not a random collection of pieces.

Wardrobe Audit

A full review of what you currently own — identifying what stays, what goes, and what's missing.

Capsule Blueprint

A precise, personalised list of every piece in your new wardrobe, with specific recommendations for each one.

Shopping Direction

Exact brands, stores, and items curated to your budget, body, and firm culture. No guesswork.

Outfit System

A clear, repeatable outfit matrix so you never stand in front of your wardrobe wondering what to wear.

Fit Guidance

Detailed guidance on fit, tailoring, and what to look for so every piece looks like it was made for you.

Follow-Up Session

A review session after you've made your purchases to ensure everything lands exactly as intended.

This engagement is available to a limited number of partners each quarter. If you're interested, the best first step is to get in touch.
